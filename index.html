Features




Just Say Hello and I'll Reply You

      Just Say Play Video and name of video you want search Eg. Play Video Shinchan

      Just Say Take a Screenshot and just enter URL of site to get its screenshot

      Just Say Weather at and name of city to get its current weather Eg. Weather at California

      Just Say news and select your country and topic of news. There are 9 awesome topics for news namely Breaking-News, World, Nation, Business, Technology, Entertainment, Sports, Science and Health.

      Just Say Play Games and Enjoy any of the four games ( 2048, Hangman, Stone Paper Scissor, Tic Tac Toe)

      Just Say Quotes and get a amazing motivational quotes with author name

      Just Say Search Book and name of the book you want to search Eg. search book harry potter and you will get 10 books to read related to search

      Just Say Tell a Joke and fill yourself with happiness

      Just Say Get Directions and enter your origin and destination, i'll guide you till Destiny.

      Just Say Get Stocks and then you can search any stock's realtime details.

      Just Say Tell Date and Get Current Date and Time

      And Still More such amazing features are coming Soon
